    About

    Who

    is ARC? We are the Athletics and Recreation Centre. This is an exciting venture for our Algonquin Commnity. This facility will provide a space for our students with a place to break through perceptual barriers in a welcoming safe, inclusive, and inviting environment for students of all backgrounds, cultures and abilities to spark a movement.

    What

    is this going to cost me? This facility will be included in your activity fee. All fee paying students can participate in all activities in the building. It will cost Algonquin College $48.8 million.

    Where

    will the ARC building be? The ARC building will be located northwest of the Student Commons building, opposite of the residence. The facility will be an estimate of 100,000 square feet! Connected to the Student Commons by a "Plus 15" Pedestrian link.
